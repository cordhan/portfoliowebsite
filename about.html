About

Cordula Hansen is a VR designer and Unity developer based in Dublin, Ireland. Prior to working full-time in VR, she worked as a lecturer and researcher in design at university level.

She has experience in immersive branding, training and educational experiences and UX design, and is currently developing a VR fintech data visualisation application with an international team.

While involved in a long-term project, she is open to remote and contract work.
