About me

Hi, I’m Cordula, and I’m a UX designer with 12 years’ experience in research and teaching design at university level, with a passionate interest in the experiences people have with objects, from archaeological artefacts to aeroplanes and digital technology.

From my research background, I have developed a talent for analysing complex information and turning it into actionable points, scheduling and seeing projects through to delivery.

I’m always looking for opportunities to collaborate with researchers, designers and developers, especially in user research and VR.
